Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Bachelor of Business Administration, popularly known as BBA, is a 3-year professional undergraduate program Affiliated with Bengaluru North University.
The Department of BBA aims to inculcate the attributes of becoming entrepreneurs, leaders, academicians, and research scholars in the field of Business Administration and Business Education. This course provides the student with conceptual, theoretical, and practical knowledge in various aspects of business - like Finance, Human Resources, Marketing, Accounting, etc. The focus is on preparing the students to tackle the challenges of today’s competitive world. Alongside, various lectures, workshops, industrial visits, conferences, Entrepreneurship Development programs, management, and cultural club activities are being held to unleash students’ creativity in different fields.

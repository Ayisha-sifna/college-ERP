Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA)

NGD has an excellent group of dedicated faculty and experts from the IT industry, guiding the students. It has an exclusive and well-equipped lab for the department with a network of computers and software’s installed. The course exposes the students to industry-relevant
subjects like DBMS, Multimedia, Data Structure, Languages like C++, JAVA, VC++, VB, Networking, Software Programming, etc.

Vision

To create a center of excellence in computer science and applications. We strive to meet the requirements of IT sectors and incorporate innovative teaching method

Mission

▪ To provide value-based technical education and training.
▪ Continuous improvement in the discipline of computer science and education.
▪ Leading the student’s community towards global excellence.
▪ Innovative teaching methods and experiential learning.

Add On Programs

▪ Cloud Computing
▪ Ethical Hacking
▪ Cyber Security
▪ Artificial Intelligence

Department Activities:

Scintilla IT Club Activity: To encourage students into academic wellbeing, club activities like coding and debugging, IT Quiz, IT gaming, web designing are organised.


BCA graduates can find their careers as a system engineer, software tester, junior programmer, web developer, system administrator, etc.
